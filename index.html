About Me

Hello! My name is Aly. I graduated from McGill University in December 2023; I studied Computer Science and Statistics. I like photography, playing guitar, and cooking!

During the summer of 2023, I interned at Morgan Stanley in Montreal as a Technology Summer Analyst. In the summer of 2022, I worked as a software developer at LIDD Consultants, a supply chain consulting company. I have experience working on backend development, integrations, end-to-end testing and monitoring tools.

Some of the upper level courses I have taken at McGill are Distributed Systems, Advanced Computer Systems, Database Systems, and Applied Machine Learning. You can find more details about my courses on my linkedin.

During 2023 and the start of 2024, I was one of the sponsorship coordinator at Hack McGill. Previously, I was the Juniors' Representative on the Computer Science Undergraduate Society (2022) and the Minors Representative at the Society of Undergraduate Mathematics Students (2021).

Pure Monte Carlo Search AI Bot

Designed a game-playing agent that uses Pure Monte Carlo Search and Minimax to create simulations to decide the best next move against an adversarial agent.

Discussion Analysis regarding COVID-19 Vaccines

This is a data science report that analyzes tweets to identify people's sentiment towards Covid-19 vaccines and the main topics surrounding the pandemic. This was a group project. My main two roles were Data Collection and Data Formatting. I also took part in refining the typology. I used Twitter's API to collect Tweets with certain Covid-related keywords (Code can be found under src/collect_tweets.py). Each tweet returned a lot of metadata, so I had to extract only the relevant data need for the project and format it in a CSV format (code is in src/json_to_csv.py).

    My other roles in the project:
  • Data annotation
  • Data analysis and discussion (Retweets and Favourite count)
  • Writing data collection section
  • Writing part of results and discussion

Hospital Finder App
